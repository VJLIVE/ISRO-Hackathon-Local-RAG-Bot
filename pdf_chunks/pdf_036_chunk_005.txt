Introduction SARAL/AltiKa is a follow-on mission to ENVISAT ESA’s mission and will have the same ground track. The satellite name SARAL stands for SAtellite for ARgos and ALtiKa. This mission continues the series of successful altimetric missions such as TOPEX/Poseidon and Jason missions.
